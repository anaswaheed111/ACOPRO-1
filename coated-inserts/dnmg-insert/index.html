DNMG Insert

DNMG Insert, positive rake angle that varies along the edge to negative in order to prevent chipping. Special design reduces cratering. Used for carbon and alloy steel, stainless steel and high temperature alloys.

  • 55° Rhombic insert
  • Double sided
  • Negative rake
  • M class
  • 11mm or 15mm cutting edges
  • Available in a range of radius for finishing, general purpose and rough turning
